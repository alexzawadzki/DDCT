About

Providing Waste Water Solutions for Over 50 Years

The Drain Doctor Inc. Is a family owned and operated Sewer and Drain Cleaning business established in 1972. Centrally located in Berlin CT, we can quickly handle all of your waste water needs across Connecticut. We offer a full line of services from residential to commercial and municipal pipe cleaning and repair, septic pumping, installation and inspection, video tracing and location, excavation, and vacuum services.

Our skilled technicians and staff are prepared to tackle any job big or small. Our wide variety of tools allow us to solve any of your waste water problems. Paying close attention to detail and using the highest quality tools we are able to efficiently solve any problems with our prompt service for both residential and municipal customers.
